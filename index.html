What is Clash Royale?

Clash Royale is a strategy game focused on collecting cards and battling others. The game makes players defend their towers with characters in the form of cards. There are two side towers and one main tower, which has a king sitting on a throne on it. Destroying a tower gives the player one crown, and the first player to destroy all towers or destroy the king's tower wins. There are also different arenas, which have different conditions from each other, allowing for different characters to be used in different arenas. Make sure to see our Best Decks per Arena section for more information. The game is ranked, with a player's rating being shown through trophies, which can be gained or lost in games. The game was developed by Supercell and released on March 2, 2016 as a Clash of Clans spinoff. The addictive gameplay and combined elements of card, tower defense, and strategy games had an immediate effect, as by 2017, the game had made 1 billion dollars. In 3 years, it had made 2.5 billion. It was also well-received by users and critics, evidenced by the data shown below.

It has also experienced a re-surge in popularity in 2025 after a decline in 2020, as content creators came back to the game, popularizing it to the masses once again and making the game reach its highest player count in years.
